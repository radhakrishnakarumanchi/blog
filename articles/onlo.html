One Not Like Other

Onlo is abbreviation of "One Thing Not Like The Other"

Does this happen to you? You think you are crystal clear about some notion. Then you come across a related notion, only then you really think clearly about both.

It has happened so often to me and it repeats for the same notions. One day, I thought I'll capture them in some way. ONLO is my word for this. I have been noting them down as #ONLO: One notion - second, contrasting notion.

Here are some examples:

  • Want - Need
  • Service - Product
  • Peformer Mode - Learner Mode
  • Cruising - Commuting
  • Listening to Respond - Listening to Understand
  • Coach - Mentor
  • Assessment - Judgement
  • Trusting others - Being Trustworthy
  • Attention - Imagination
  • Onstage - BackStage
  • Calendar - Email
  • Maker's Schedule - Manager's Schedule
  • Standard of Living - Quality of Life
  • Rhythm - Discipline
