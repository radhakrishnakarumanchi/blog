“Today, there is Trump; tomorrow, there will be Bump”

I attended Mahabharata Immersion workshop in Kotagiri in February this year. I got a lot from the workshop.

I would like to relate a particular interaction between a participant and Raghu, the main facilitator, as it struck a chord with me.

Participant: “Raghu, this is all well and good. In the setting of this workshop, we can sit in a circle and talk and be more of who we want to be. But after the 5 days are over and we go back to the real world, they will not understand all of this. How can I apply any of this out in the real world”

Raghu: “It is us who have to change. The world will not change for you. Today, there is Trump. Tomorrow, there will be Bump. It is upto each of us if and how we change”
